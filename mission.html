Mission

Our goal is to encourage others to be proactive about their own health and well-being through visual media. We think it is important for people to stay educated about advances in medicine and preventive measures, and we believe that visuals such as infographics are especially effective in conveying this type of information.

What We Aim For

Education

We wish to educate. We provide easy to read infographics to educate the average citizen in everyday health matters as well as blossoming doctors in their studies. We provide infographics from many different categories (from General Health to General Science) as part of a bigger effort to provide and inspire education.

Outreach

We wish to distribute free resources to educate others with no opportunity for themselves. We provide an easy to reach and easy to use site with easy to access infographics that are easy to read in order to facilitate the access of our plentiful supplies.

Community

We wish to produce a community of caring and educated individuals who inspire on another to learn and take the next step, whether it to be in pursuing a medical career, or just to provide general health education to friends and family. An ounce of prevention is a pound of cure, and the source of prevention is a great community providing education and aid.
